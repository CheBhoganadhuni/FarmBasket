Order Cancellation

We understand that plans change! Here is how our cancellation works:

  • You can cancel your order directly from the Order Details page at any time before it is marked as Shipped.
  • Once the order status changes to Shipped or Out for Delivery, cancellation is no longer possible.
  • If you missed the cancellation window, please contact our support team immediately.

Refund Policy

All refunds are processed to your FarmBasket Wallet.

  • Timeline: Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after the cancellation is approved.
  • Wallet Usage: The refunded amount will be added to your FarmBasket Wallet balance, which can be used for 100% of the value of your future purchases.
  • Non-Transferable: Please note that Wallet balance cannot be transferred to a bank account.

Returns & Damaged Items

We take great care in packing your organic produce. However, if something goes wrong:

  • If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please report it within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Email us at support@farmbasket.com with photos of the affected item.
  • Upon verification, we will issue a full refund to your wallet or arrange a replacement.
