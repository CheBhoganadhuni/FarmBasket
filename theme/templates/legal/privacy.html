Account Deletion & Data Handling

At FarmBasket, we understand that you may want to deactivate or remove your account. We have slightly different procedures for each method to ensure data integrity and security.

1. Account Deactivation (Self-Service)

When you choose to "Delete Account" from your profile "Danger Zone", we perform a Soft Delete (Deactivation). This means your account is marked as inactive, and you will be logged out immediatey.
What happens:

  • You can no longer log in.
  • Your profile is hidden.
  • Your past order history is preserved for our legal and accounting records.

2. Permanent Data Erasure

If you wish to have your data permanently wiped from our servers (Right to be Forgotten), this requires a manual process to ensure no pending orders or legal obligations exist.
Please contact our support team to request a permanent deletion.
